# 🔗 Change Your Vercel Domain Name

You're in **Settings** but need to go to **Domains** section.

---

## Step-by-Step

1. **Go back** to your project dashboard
   - URL: `https://vercel.com/dashboard`
   - Click your **portfolio** project

2. **Look at the left sidebar** and find:
   - **Deployments** (top)
   - **Settings** (where you are now)
   - **Domains** ← **CLICK HERE** ⭐

3. **In Domains section**, you'll see:
   ```
   portfolio-h26eaheu8-gunjans-projects-a4148da6.vercel.app
   ```

4. **Click the three dots (⋮)** on the right of that domain

5. **Select "Edit"**

6. **Change the name** to one of these:
   - `gunjanmehta` (becomes: gunjanmehta.vercel.app)
   - `portfolio-gunjan` (becomes: portfolio-gunjan.vercel.app)
   - `gmehta` (becomes: gmehta.vercel.app)

7. **Click "Save"**

✅ **Done!** New domain is live in seconds.
