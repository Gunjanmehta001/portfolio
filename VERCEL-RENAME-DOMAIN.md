# 🔗 Rename Your Vercel Domain

Your current URL: `https://portfolio-h26eaheu8-gunjans-projects-a4148da6.vercel.app/`

Rename to something cleaner like: `https://gunjanmehta.vercel.app/` or `https://portfolio-gunjan.vercel.app/`

---

## Step 1: Go to Vercel Dashboard

1. Visit: https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Click on your **portfolio** project

---

## Step 2: Access Settings

1. Click **Settings** (top navigation)
2. Go to **Domains** section (left sidebar)

---

## Step 3: Rename Default Domain

You'll see your current domain listed. Click the **three dots** (⋮) next to it:

Options:
- **Edit** → Change the subdomain name
- **Remove** → Delete it
- **Set as Primary** → Make it your main domain

---

## Step 4: Edit Domain Name

Click **Edit**:

**Current**: `portfolio-h26eaheu8-gunjans-projects-a4148da6.vercel.app`

**Choose new name** (examples):
- `gunjanmehta.vercel.app` ← Recommended (your name)
- `portfolio-gunjan.vercel.app`
- `gunjan-portfolio.vercel.app`
- `gmehta-portfolio.vercel.app`

**Check availability** → Click **Save**

---

## Step 5: Update Complete ✅

Your new domain is live immediately!

Example:
- **Old**: `https://portfolio-h26eaheu8-gunjans-projects-a4148da6.vercel.app/`
- **New**: `https://gunjanmehta.vercel.app/`

---

## Add Custom Domain (Optional)

Want your own domain? (e.g., `gunjanmehta.com`)

1. In **Domains** section
2. Click **Add Domain**
3. Enter your domain (must own it)
4. Update DNS records at your registrar
5. Vercel handles SSL automatically ✓
